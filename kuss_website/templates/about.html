The KUSS Constitution

The founding document that guides our mission, structure, and values.

Preamble

"We, the members of the Kabale University Surgical Society, united by a common dedication to the advancement of surgical knowledge and practice, establish this society to promote excellence in the field of surgery through education, research, and mentorship."

Our Vision

"To achieve a society that nurtures future holistic surgeons."

Our Mission

To equip society members with the capacity and opportunity to foster their aspirations for a future surgical career through surgical education, research, and mentorship.

Our Motto

"Supra et Ultra"

Above and Beyond

Our Objectives

As outlined in Article 3 of the Constitution, the objectives of the Society are:

1. To advocate, develop and maintain high ethical professional standards among members.
2. To provide opportunities for medical students to be exposed to the surgical field.
3. To promote the development of skills necessary for advancing surgical techniques.
4. To promote a high standard of surgical care.
5. To liaise with other surgical organizations countrywide and worldwide to create global exchange opportunities.
6. To establish a mentorship programme between medical students and identified mentoring surgeons.
7. To conduct and partake in research for the advancement of surgery.
8. To establish a journal club and surgical skills sessions for members.

The Secretariat

The Secretariat of the Society shall be located at the Department of Surgery, Kabale University School of Medicine, Kabale, Uganda.

Member Rights & Obligations

All members have the right to attend all society functions, vote in elections, access society resources, and participate in mentorship programs. Members are obligated to abide by the constitution, pay subscription fees, uphold the society's reputation, and actively participate in society activities.

The Executive Committee

The day-to-day management body of the Society, elected annually at the AGM. Composed of five key officers:

  • Chairperson: Presides over all meetings and represents the Society.
  • Vice Chairperson: Assists the Chairperson and oversees committees.
  • General Secretary: Handles correspondence, records, and communications.
  • Treasurer: Manages all finances and prepares financial reports.
  • Publicity Secretary: Handles media, communications, and event promotion.

Meetings

Annual General Meeting (AGM)

Held once per academic year. All members attend. Elections are conducted, reports are presented, and major decisions are made.

Executive Meetings

Held at least once per month. The Executive Committee discusses operational matters and prepares for larger meetings.

Emergency General Meeting (EGM)

Convened by the Chairperson or upon request by two-thirds of the Executive when urgent matters arise.

The KUSS Oath

As stated in Appendix II of the Constitution, every member takes the following oath upon joining:

"I, _______________________, being admitted into the Kabale University Surgical Society, do solemnly swear that I will uphold the Constitution of this Society, dedicate myself to the pursuit of surgical excellence, respect my colleagues and mentors, and strive always to go Supra et Ultra — Above and Beyond — in all my endeavors.

So help me God."
